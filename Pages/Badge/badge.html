Badge

Badges are small circles that are used to convey number of notifications received or the online/offline status of an individual.

Profile Badge

Profile badges are small circle at the bottom right of an individual's avatar that used to convey the online/offline status.

It has three variants online | dnd | offline

Number Badge

Number badge is used to show the number of notifications or the number of items present in the parent component.
